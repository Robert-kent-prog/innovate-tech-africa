Developed custom bulk SMS platforms for Gospel Outreach Church Kyuso and African Inland Church Kwavonza in Mwingi North. The systems enable efficient member communication, event notifications, and important announcements.

  • Mass SMS broadcasting to church members
  • Member grouping and segmentation
  • Scheduled message delivery
  • Delivery reports and analytics
  • User-friendly admin interface
